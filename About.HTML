About

Our Photography services is a collaboration between Keegan Flahive and Lauren Esh. We are a small photography business located in Lititz, Pennsylvania. We are here to capture pictures that will last a lifetime. We specialize in aerial, portrait, and landscape photography. Take a look below to learn a little more about Keegan and Lauren.



Keegan Flahive

I am currently a Senior at Harrisburg University where I study Geo-spatial technology (GIS).I have been working for my dads business ( Argos UAS ) as drone pilot since 2016 and have conducted hundreds of projects utilizing photography both in the air and on the ground. I spend my free time traveling and photographing everything I can with my camera and drone. Love to watch college football and play video games with friends when its a rainy day and I cannot be outside.


Lauren Flahive

I am currently a senior at Millersville University, where I study Elementary Education and Special Education. Family is everything to me! My favorite places to vacation are at my family cabin and the beach. During my spare time I love crafting, painting, and making home decor signs. My favorite time of year is summer and spending endless days and nights by the pool.
